# Site brief

## Public-facing name
International Plant Pangenome Network

## Positioning
A clean, professional, lightweight website for an international research network in plant pangenomics.

## Core pages
- Home
- How we work
- People
- Contact us

## Design goals
- polished but simple
- spacious and typographically strong
- academic and credible
- not corporate
- not startup-like
- ready for GitHub Pages

## Structural notes
- reserve space for a future network logo
- reserve space for a future Earlham Institute logo
- keep text editable in `docs/copy/`
- use a contact form rather than exposing a direct email by default

## Hosting line
Hosted by the Earlham Institute
